Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our authentication system.

Account Management

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar. Fill out the registration form with your email address, username, and a secure password. After submitting the form, you'll receive a verification email. Click on the verification link to activate your account.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" button and then select "Forgot Password?" Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click on the link in the email to set a new password.

After logging in, navigate to your profile by clicking on your username in the navigation bar. From your profile page, click on the "Edit Profile" button. Here, you can update your personal information, change your profile picture, and save your changes.

Security

Yes, we take security very seriously. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your information. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. We also implement regular security audits to ensure your data remains protected.

To enhance your account security, we recommend:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Enabling two-factor authentication in your account settings
  • Regularly updating your password
  • Not sharing your login credentials with others
  • Logging out when using shared computers

Technical Issues

If you haven't received a verification email, please check your spam or junk folder first. If you still can't find it, you can request a new verification email by logging in to your account and following the prompt to resend the verification. If problems persist, please contact our support team.

If you're experiencing slow loading times, try:

  • Clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Using a different web browser
  • Checking your internet connection
  • Disabling any ad-blocking extensions temporarily
If the issue persists, it might be due to high traffic or temporary server issues. Please try again later or contact our support team.

Privacy

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services, including your name, email address, username, and any other information you choose to provide in your profile. We also collect standard log data, such as IP addresses and browser information. For complete details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

To delete your account, go to your account settings and select the "Delete Account" option. Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion. Once deleted, all your personal data will be removed from our active databases. Some information may remain in our backups for a limited time, but will not be accessible or used. If you need assistance, please contact our support team.
